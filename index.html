Guess the MLB Team!

Press letter keys on the keyboard to choose a letter. If the chosen letter is in the team name, it will be displayed to help you determine the name of the team. In the event that half of the guesses are used, a hint will be displayed to help determine the team name. Upon guessing all of the correct letters within the team's name, the logo of that team will be displayed and the game will supply a new team name for the user to guess.
